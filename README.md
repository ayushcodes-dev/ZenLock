# 🔐 ZenLock — Secure. Simple. Smart.

**ZenLock** is a clean and modern **password manager** designed to make security effortless.  
It helps you **store, generate, and manage passwords** safely — all within a simple and elegant interface.  

Because managing passwords shouldn’t be stressful — it should feel Zen. 🌿

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔒 **Secure Storage** — All your passwords are encrypted and stored locally on your device for maximum privacy.  
- ⚙️ **Smart Password Generator** — Instantly create strong, random, and unique passwords.  
- 📋 **One-Click Copy** — Copy passwords or usernames instantly and safely.  
- 💾 **Local-First** — Your data never leaves your device.  
- 📱 **Responsive Design** — Works beautifully on desktop, tablet, and mobile screens.  
- ⚡ **Blazing Fast** — Built with **React** and **Tailwind CSS** for performance and simplicity.

---

## 🧠 Why ZenLock?

We live in a world full of logins and passwords.  
ZenLock brings calm to that chaos — helping you keep everything **secure, organised, and easy to access** without relying on third-party cloud storage.  

> *Your data. Your device. Your control.*

---

## 🧩 Tech Stack

- ⚛️ **React.js** — Frontend framework  
- 🎨 **Tailwind CSS** — Modern utility-first styling  
- 💾 **LocalStorage API** — For offline data storage  
- 🛠️ **Vite / Next.js** (depending on setup) — Fast development & build tools
